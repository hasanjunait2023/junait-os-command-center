# Junait OS Module Map

## A-to-Z module inventory

### A. Admin Command Center
Founder dashboard, command routing, quick actions.

### B. Automation Control
All automated routines, cron jobs, webhook flows, status.

### C. Business Operations
Garments, wholesale, SaaS, digital products, LMS operational status.

### D. Documents and Knowledge
Contracts, SOPs, notes, plans, reference docs.

### E. Executive Review
Weekly and monthly decision review system.

### F. Finance and Cashflow
Revenue, expenses, subscriptions, due payments, runway, snapshots.

### G. Growth and Leads
Lead sources, conversions, follow-ups, pipeline status.

### H. Hosting and Infrastructure
Domains, DNS, VPS, deployments, uptime, incidents.

### I. Integrations Registry
Which systems are connected, healthy, stale, or pending.

### J. Jobs and Execution Queue
Current tasks, owners, blockers, delivery status.

### K. Knowledge Intelligence
Research summaries, market notes, project intelligence.

### L. LMS and Students
Student activity, learning ops, updates, issues.

### M. Messaging and Communications
Telegram, email, alerts, support threads, outreach.

### N. Notifications and Alerts
Critical alerts, digest alerts, anomaly alerts.

### O. Offers and Products
SaaS modules, digital offers, launches, landing pages.

### P. Projects and Delivery
Roadmaps, active projects, milestones, deadlines.

### Q. Quality and Risk
QA results, release gates, operational risk register.

### R. Research and Market Intel
Watchlists, competitor notes, market changes.

### S. Social and Content
Content calendar, distribution status, channel health.

### T. Trading and Performance
Trading logs, weekly performance, discipline review, signal health.

### U. Users and CRM
Contacts, customers, segments, opportunities.

### V. Visibility and Analytics
Topline dashboards, trends, snapshots, anomalies.

### W. Websites and Domains
Site health, DNS, SSL, landing pages, uptime.

### X. Experimental Lab
New ideas, AI experiments, prototypes.

### Y. Yearly Targets
Quarterly and annual goals.

### Z. Zero Inbox / Reset Systems
Cleanup routines, stale task review, unfinished loop cleanup.
